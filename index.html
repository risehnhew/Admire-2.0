Welcome

Welcome to the official website for AdMIRe 2.0, an expanded task for interpreting potentially idiomatic expressions (PIEs) across text and visual modalities. Understanding non-compositional language remains a major challenge in NLP.

Building on the foundations of SemEval-2025 Task 1 (AdMIRe), this new iteration introduces key updates, including the addition of verbal PIEs and broader language coverage with French, Arabic, Turkish, Chinese, Portuguese, and German. We aim to advance multi-lingual and multi-modal evaluation of idiomatic language understanding.

Task Description

The task is composed of two multimodal subtasks. Participants can choose to participate in one or both.

Subtask A: Static Image Ranking

Given a context sentence with a potentially idiomatic expression (PIE), participants must rank a set of 5 images according to how well they visually capture the idiomatic meaning within that specific context.

Subtask B: Image Sequence Completion

In this subtask, participants are given a target expression and an incomplete image sequence (like a comic strip). The objective is to select the most appropriate image to conclude the visual sequence, which could represent either the idiomatic or literal meaning of the expression.
